Implementation Tips
Content Balance

Maintain a healthy mix across all three categories:

  • HERO: 10-20% of content
  • HUB: 60-70% of content
  • HELP: 10-20% of content
Audience Focus

Each type serves different audience needs:

  • HERO: Broad reach & awareness
  • HUB: Engagement & loyalty
  • HELP: Support & conversion
